About Me

I dedicated years working in the healthcare sector caring for others, but after experiencing the toll of stress and burnout, I made the difficult decision to step away from my profession in May 2024.

While searching for a new path that could reignite my sense of joy and purpose, I discovered the world of software development.
Drawn by its creativity, problem-solving opportunities, and potential to make a positive impact, I am now embarking on this exciting journey.

With my background in healthcare, creative mindset and a passion for learning, I am eager to explore how I can combine the empathy and precision cultivated through years of patient care, the problem solving honed in high-pressure medical environments, and the innovative thinking inspired by my journey into software development to create and deliver meaningful solutions in the tech space.

My professional background and certificates

After earning my nursing degree in Italy in 2016, I took a leap of faith and moved to England in 2017 to embark on my healthcare career.
Over the years, I worked across various roles in the healthcare sector, progressing from a Healthcare Assistant at Band 2 to a Registered Nurse at Band 5. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I faced an unexpected yet deeply personal challenge. With my family in Italy, I made the difficult decision to temporarily step away from my career in England and return home. For approximately six to eight months, I focused on what mattered most—being there for my loved ones during a time of uncertainty.
